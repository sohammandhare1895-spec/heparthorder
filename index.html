Card Based Payments

Credit Cards

Allows consumers to borrow funds from a pre-approved limit (e.g., Visa, Mastercard, American Express).

Debit Cards

Deducts money directly from the user's bank account instantly.

Prepaid/Gift Cards

Pre-loaded cards with a specific amount of money, often used for limited, secure spending.

Digital & Mobile Wallets

E-Wallets

Digital applications that store payment information, offering fast, contactless, and 1-click checkout options (e.g., Apple Pay, Google Pay, PayPal, Paytm, Alipay).

QR Code Payments

Mobile scanning of QR codes to pay directly from a linked bank account or wallet (e.g., UPI in India).

Bank Based Payments

Bank Transfers (ACH/NEFT/RTGS)

Direct electronic transfers between bank accounts, commonly used for high-value payments.

Direct Debit

Automatic, recurring, pre-authorized deductions from a bank account.

Net Banking

Direct payment through a bank's official website or portal.

Alternative Payments

Buy Now, Pay Later (BNPL)

Short-term financing that allows customers to make purchases immediately and pay in installments, usually interest-free (e.g., Klarna, Afterpay).

Cryptocurrency

Decentralized digital currencies that use blockchain technology for fast, borderless transactions (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum).

Cash

Physical paper banknotes and coins, still used heavily for in-person transactions.

Cheques

Paper-based, written directives for transferring funds, which are declining in popularity.

Real-time Payments (RTPs)

Instant, irreversible inter-bank payments.

Emerging Technologies

Biometrics

Payments secured by facial recognition or fingerprint.

Tokenised Payments

Secure payments where sensitive card information is replaced by a unique digital identifier (token).
